Applying under aid for start-ups

Based on the amount of money you're applying for, your minimum company contribution is:

10%

This is the minimum contribution you need to make towards your eligible project costs. (This is referred to throughout the application as 'match funding')

The maximum amount of public funding you can apply for towards your total eligible project costs is:

90%

! Warning You will need these figures to complete the project cost breakdown form in the next step
